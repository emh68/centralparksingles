FAQs

SPONSOR

Q: Who is sponsoring/organizing this conference? Is this an official church activity?
A: The Central Park Singles Conference is an official church activity sponsored by the Central Park Mid-Singles Ward.

COST

Q: Does the conference cost anything?
A: No, this conference is free! However, we do ask that you please make sure to register as early as possible so we can plan for enough food and space to accomodate everyone who is coming. There will be some food/meals provided, but any additional food/meals, lodging, gas, plane tickets, etc. are your individual responsibility.

AGE & ATTENDANCE

Q: Who can attend this conference? Is there an age range?
A: The conference is intended for ALL SINGLES between the ages of 31 - 45 years old.

LOCATION

Q: Where is this located?
A: Currently, all activities related to the conference will be held at the Central Park Mid-Singles Ward church building. If this changes we will let you know. The Central Park Mid-Singles Ward of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints is located at:
1155 1st St., Idaho Falls, ID 83401

Housing/Lodging & Transportation

Q: Will the conference provide any type of housing, lodging or transportation?
A: We cannot arrange for or plan for your personal travel, lodging and transportation to and from the conference as well as during the conference. If you would like to make arrangements to carpool or for transportation and housing/lodging you can post on the Facebook Event Page and try to make arrangements with others.
